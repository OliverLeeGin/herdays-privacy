HerDays Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2023

Introduction

HerDays ("we" or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our period tracking application. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring your information is kept private and secure.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Menstrual cycle data (period start and end dates)
  • Symptoms and health notes you choose to record
  • Mood and physical sensations
  • Activity and lifestyle information you provide
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage data to improve our services

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide period predictions and cycle insights
  • Generate personalized health recommendations
  • Improve the functionality and user experience of our app
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and ensure proper functioning
  • Develop new features based on user patterns and feedback

Your data is primarily processed on your device. Any data transmitted to our servers is used only for the purposes stated in this policy.

Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously. Here's how we protect your information:

  • All data is encrypted during transmission using industry-standard protocols
  • We implement secure storage practices for any data stored on our servers
  • Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized personnel
  • We regularly review and update our security practices
  • Personal health data is stored primarily on your device when possible

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • If required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent, if you choose to share your data

Anonymous, aggregated data may be used for research or statistical purposes.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access: You can request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: You can request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Right to deletion: You can request deletion of your data from our servers
  • Right to restrict processing: You can request limits on how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object: You can object to certain types of processing

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information provided below.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you maintain an active account. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we need to retain certain information for legitimate business or legal purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete this information immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
